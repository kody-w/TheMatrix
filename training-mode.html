Tips, Tricks & Hints

Master the fundamentals of PvP gameplay.

Energy Management

  • ‚ö° Fast moves generate energy each turn. Plan when you'll have enough for charged moves.
  • ‚ö° Different fast moves gain different energy rates. Vine Whip gains 4, Water Gun gains 5.
  • ‚ö° Switch out to "bank" energy on the next Pokemon. Energy carries over to switched Pokemon.
  • ‚ö° Deny opponent energy by switching when they're close to a charged move.

Shield Usage

  • üõ°Ô∏è You have 2 shields per battle (in most formats). Use them strategically!
  • üõ°Ô∏è Save shields for moves that would KO you or flip the matchup.
  • üõ°Ô∏è Bait shields with weak moves when opponent has full shields remaining.
  • üõ°Ô∏è Once shields are gone, play more cautiously. Any charged move can threaten a KO.

Move Selection

  • üéØ Pick charged moves with different coverage. Don't redundant super-effective coverage.
  • üéØ Balance fast move for damage output vs. charged move buildup speed.
  • üéØ Consider move animation duration when predicting opponent timing.
  • üéØ Legacy moves can have unique coverage. Check what's available for your Pokemon.

Type Coverage

  • üìä Every Pokemon has strengths and weaknesses. Build teams with synergy.
  • üìä Dual-type Pokemon have more complex matchups. Account for both types!
  • üìä Super-effective coverage beats defensive stats. Good coverage wins games.
  • üìä Some types are "always good" (water, grass). Others are situational.

Advanced Tactics

  • üéÆ CMP (Attack stat tiebreaker) decides who goes first when both charge. Higher attack wins!
  • üéÆ Swap-in mechanics: Switching costs 2 turns. Plan swaps carefully!
  • üéÆ Baiting: Force shields by throwing weak moves. Then KO with powered-up move.
  • üéÆ Wall/Pivot Pokemon: Use them to safely check threats and farm down opponents.
